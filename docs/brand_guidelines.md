# FincaGrown Aesthetics & Photography Guidelines

This document tracks the specific compositional styles used for generated FincaGrown / Pata Palo assets to ensure a highly uniform, premium e-commerce layout where all images blend perfectly with the `mix-blend-mode: multiply` CSS layout.

## 1. Fresh Produce (Frutas y Vegetales)
*Reference Image:* `images/jackfruit.png`
- **Lighting:** Studio-lit, soft, clean light.
- **Placement:** Completely isolated on a pure, solid, flat white background (#ffffff). NO tables, NO wood, NO shadows across the background wall.
- **Subject Angle:** The fresh produce must never be standing perfectly straight. It must be resting casually at a natural diagonal/slanted tilt.
- **Grounding/Shadow:** A soft, realistic contact shadow MUST exist directly underneath the fruit to anchor it.

## 2. Flours & Powders (Harinas y Polvos)
*Reference Image:* `images/harina-camote.png`
- **Lighting:** Studio-lit, delicate light highlighting the powder texture.
- **Placement:** Completely isolated on a pure, solid, flat white background (#ffffff). NO tables.
- **Subject Angle/Form:** The powders are typically presented as organic, beautifully textured mounds or piles (often with a rustic wooden spoon buried slightly into it, or just a very aesthetic loose pile).
- **Grounding/Shadow:** Similar soft, realistic contact shadow directly underneath the pile or spoon to ground the composition against the pure white backdrop.

## 3. Packaged Goods (Empaquetados // Ej. Jackfruit Empacado, Pastillas)
- **Style:** Premium matte-finish stand-up pouches (e.g., olive green or deep blue depending on line).
- **Branding:** Contains the official Pata Palo organic circular sticker.
- **Placement:** Isolated on white, standing dynamically with a soft contact shadow or slight reflection.
